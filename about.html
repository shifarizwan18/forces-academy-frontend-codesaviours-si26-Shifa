Our History

Academy Overview

Established with the vision of grooming young individuals for a bright future in the Armed Forces, Forces Academy Faisalabad has a proud legacy of discipline, dedication and excellence.

Over the years, we have developed a structured training environment that combines academic rigor, physical training and character building to shape future leaders of the nation.
