Getting Started

How To Apply

Five steps from application to orientation day.

  1. 01
    Fill The Application Form

    Collect the form from our Canal Road campus or download it from the Student Portal, and fill in your personal and academic details.

  2. 02
    Submit Documents For Verification

    Bring the required documents listed below to our admissions desk, where our team will verify and register your application.

  3. 03
    Attend The Entry Assessment

    Sit for a short screening interview and academic assessment so we can place you in the right batch and course.

  4. 04
    Pay The Admission Fee

    Deposit the registration and first month's fee according to the fee structure to confirm your seat.

  5. 05
    Receive Confirmation & Attend Orientation

    Get your admission confirmation and welcome pack, then join the orientation day to meet your instructors and batch.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

ISSB Preparation
  • Already have an ISSB call letter, or plan to apply soon
  • Minimum qualification: Intermediate (FSc / ICS / A-Level)
  • Physically and mentally fit for training
PMA Long Course Prep
  • Age between 17 and 22 years on course start date
  • Unmarried male candidates, FSc / ICS / A-Level pass
  • Meets Army height & medical requirements
Short & Crash Courses
  • Open to any serious aspirant, no test call letter required
  • Matric or above, any recognized board
  • Ideal for early or last-minute preparation

Exact criteria can vary slightly by intake. Visit the campus or call our admissions desk to confirm eligibility for your specific case.

Before You Visit

Required Documents

Keep the following documents ready — original and one photocopy each — to make your admissions desk visit quick and smooth.

Missing a document? Bring what you have and our admissions team will guide you on the rest.

  • CNIC (or B-Form for under 18) — original and copy
  • 4 recent passport-size photographs
  • Educational certificates and mark sheets (Matric / FSc / equivalent)
  • Domicile certificate
  • School / college leaving or character certificate
  • Medical fitness certificate (for PMA Long Course candidates)
