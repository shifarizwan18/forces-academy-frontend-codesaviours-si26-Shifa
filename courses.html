    Choose Your Path

    Whether you're starting Matric or walking into your ISSB interview next month, there's a course built for exactly where you stand today.

    Intermediate

    FSc Pre-Medical

    Biology, Chemistry and Physics coursework built for board exams alongside MDCAT groundwork, so students don't start from zero after Intermediate.

    • Duration: 2 Years (11th & 12th)
    • For: Matric Science students aiming for medical college or ISSB
    Learn More
    Intermediate

    FSc Pre-Engineering

    Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry taught with an engineering-entry-test mindset, preparing students for ECAT and PMA academics at the same time.

    • Duration: 2 Years (11th & 12th)
    • For: Matric Science students aiming for engineering university or PMA
    Learn More
    Intermediate

    ICS (Computer Science)

    Computer Science, Mathematics and Physics with hands-on programming labs, for students who want a technology-focused Intermediate.

    • Duration: 2 Years (11th & 12th)
    • For: Matric students interested in software, IT or technical Forces branches
    Learn More
    Matriculation

    Matric Science

    A structured 9th & 10th grade science program covering Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics — the foundation every FSc course depends on.

    • Duration: 2 Years (9th & 10th)
    • For: Students who've completed Class 8 and want a strong science base
    Learn More
    Matriculation

    Matric Arts / Humanities

    Pakistan Studies, Islamiat, Civics and Economics taught with the same discipline as the science wing, for students choosing the humanities stream.

    • Duration: 2 Years (9th & 10th)
    • For: Students who've completed Class 8 and prefer arts / humanities
    Learn More
    Test Preparation

    ISSB Preparation Course

    Intensive coaching for the two-stage ISSB process — psychological tests, group tasks, planning exercises and the personal interview — run by experienced instructors.

    • Duration: 6–8 Weeks (Intensive)
    • For: Candidates who already hold or expect an ISSB call letter
    Learn More
    Test Preparation

    PMA Long Course Entry Test Prep

    Academic test prep, physical conditioning and interview practice built specifically around the Pakistan Military Academy Kakul entry requirements.

    • Duration: 4 Months
    • For: Male candidates targeting the PMA Long Course
    Learn More
    Test Preparation

    Entry Test Preparation (MDCAT / ECAT)

    Subject-wise MCQ practice, timed mock tests and weak-area analysis for the national medical and engineering entry tests.

    • Duration: 6 Months
    • For: FSc Pre-Medical / Pre-Engineering graduates
    Learn More
    Bridging Program

    O/A Level Bridging Program

    Converts Cambridge O/A Level coursework into the local FSc/Matric syllabus and ISSB-style testing format, so no student falls behind on entry tests.

    • Duration: Flexible (3–6 Months)
    • For: O/A Level students moving into local-curriculum entry tests
    Learn More
